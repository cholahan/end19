Geocoding Early Novels

Geocoding Early Novels is a project conceived in 2019 that maps specific locations mentioned in the title pages of novels from the long eighteenth century. Our dataset of 2,002 novels from 1660-1850 is from the Early Novels Database project at the Kislak Center in the University of Pennsylvania. You can learn more about that database project here.

In addition to mapping these novels based on their locations, we have also created maps based on publication date, the authorâ€™s gender, narrative form, etc. You can find the maps here.
